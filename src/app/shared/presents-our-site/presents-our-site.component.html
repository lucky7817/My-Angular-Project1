What is Be Gallery?

We give way ...

Buddy Gallery tries to present different in every way paintings made only and only by hand, by famous and unknown artists from all over the world. We empower people with drawing skills to showcase themselves to our online audience and thus turn their talent, skill and imagination into their business and their pride and self-esteem!

Our audience….

If you have a passion for handmade paintings, we are your artistic and aesthetic oasis, where we hope you will find what you are looking for, to make your home more beautiful, to complete your collection of paintings or simply to make a very unique and beautiful gift to the person next to you, to your friend or colleague or to a member of your family.

Your doubts….

If you happen to have doubts about the authenticity of the picture you have purchased, you can take advantage of our fourteen-day return period and a subsequent refund of the amount you have paid.
